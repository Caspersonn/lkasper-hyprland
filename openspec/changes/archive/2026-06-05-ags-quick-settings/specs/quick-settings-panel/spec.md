## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Control center PopupWindow overlay
The implementation in `ags/windows/quick-settings/index.tsx` SHALL provide a PopupWindow overlay (full-screen transparent outer area, inner panel aligned top-right) that serves as the unified control center.

#### Scenario: Open panel from right-pill trigger icon
- **GIVEN** the bar in `ags/windows/bar/right-pill.tsx` is visible
- **WHEN** the user clicks Bluetooth, WiFi, Volume, CPU, or Battery icon
- **THEN** the system SHALL open the control center panel

#### Scenario: Do not open panel from tray or bell
- **GIVEN** the right pill is visible
- **WHEN** the user clicks the tray area or notification bell
- **THEN** the system SHALL NOT open the control center panel

### Requirement: Panel close behavior
The control center SHALL support explicit and implicit dismissal via PopupWindow behavior.

#### Scenario: Close on outside click
- **GIVEN** the control center is open
- **WHEN** the user clicks outside the inner panel content area
- **THEN** the system SHALL close the panel

#### Scenario: Close on Escape key
- **GIVEN** the control center is open
- **WHEN** the user presses Escape
- **THEN** the system SHALL close the panel

#### Scenario: Toggle close from trigger icon
- **GIVEN** the control center is open
- **WHEN** the user clicks a quick-settings trigger icon again
- **THEN** the system SHALL close the panel

### Requirement: Control center layout
The inner panel SHALL be structured vertically with four zones: QuickActions row, Tiles grid, Sliders, and inline Pages areas.

#### Scenario: Render panel layout
- **GIVEN** the control center panel is opened
- **WHEN** the inner panel is rendered
- **THEN** the layout SHALL contain QuickActions at top, followed by Tiles container, followed by Sliders container, with Pages Revealers embedded in the Tiles and Sliders zones

### Requirement: Trigger highlight state
When the panel is open, trigger icons in `ags/windows/bar/right-pill.tsx` SHALL show an active highlight using a lighter shade of the pill background, as generated by `ags/services/theme.ts`.

#### Scenario: Apply highlight while panel is open
- **GIVEN** the control center is open
- **WHEN** the trigger icons are rendered
- **THEN** Bluetooth, WiFi, Volume, CPU, and Battery icons SHALL display the highlighted state

#### Scenario: Clear highlight when panel closes
- **GIVEN** the control center has just closed
- **WHEN** the right pill is rendered
- **THEN** trigger icons SHALL return to their normal non-highlighted style

### Requirement: Right-pill ordering includes bell at far right
The right pill layout in `ags/windows/bar/right-pill.tsx` SHALL place the notification bell after Battery as the rightmost item.

#### Scenario: Render right-pill module order
- **GIVEN** the bar is rendered
- **WHEN** the right pill contents are shown
- **THEN** the order SHALL include Battery immediately before the notification bell and no item after the bell
